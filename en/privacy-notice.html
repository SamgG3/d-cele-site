Privacy Notice

Last updated: 26.04.2026

This privacy notice has been prepared to inform visitors of the Daban-Cêlê website about the current data-processing scope of the site.

The website is currently structured as a brand showcase, lookbook, and editorial information space. It does not offer account creation, checkout, payment processing, order placement, shipment tracking, returns, exchanges, or identity-based application flows.

Scope of data not requested on-site: The website does not request or store full name, government identity number, delivery address, payment data, card information, order history, or sensitive personal data through on-site forms.

External communication channels: Links to WhatsApp, phone, and future social or email channels simply redirect the visitor to third-party services. Any information voluntarily shared on those external platforms is governed by their own privacy terms.

Purpose of the current structure: The website exists to present the brand, its collections, its editorial direction, and direct contact routes. It does not build user profiles, process commercial orders, or operate a customer database through on-site form submission.

Data transfer: Because the site does not maintain an internal personal-data collection flow, Daban-Cêlê does not perform bulk transfers of visitor-submitted personal data through this website structure.

Visitor rights: If the site structure changes in the future and introduces account, payment, application, or form-based data collection processes, this notice will be updated accordingly and clearly republished.
