Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26.04.2026

This policy explains what the Daban-Cêlê website currently does and does not collect within its present showcase-based structure.

Information not collected through the site: The website does not request or store names, telephone numbers, email addresses, delivery details, identity information, payment data, or customer-account information through on-site forms or account creation tools.

No behavioural marketing tracking: The site does not use advertising pixels, retargeting systems, or profiling-based marketing infrastructure designed to commercially analyse visitor behaviour.

External platforms: When a visitor chooses to move to WhatsApp, a phone dialer, or future email or social platforms, the relevant third-party service controls any further processing according to its own rules and policies.

Local preference storage: A cookie notice preference may be stored only on the visitor's own device through local storage so the notice is not shown repeatedly. That local preference is not treated as a customer profile or transmitted as a marketing record.

Content nature: All product references, collection imagery, story elements, and editorial texts are published for brand presentation and informational purposes. The site does not perform automated decision-making, scoring, or commercial user profiling.

If the structure changes in the future and introduces wider data-processing features, this privacy policy will be revised and republished with updated scope information.
