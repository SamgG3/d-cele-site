Press & Partnerships

Partnerships

An open but selective ground for collaborations aligned with the brand

Editorial shoots, brand partnerships, styling projects, content collaborations, and selected media requests are reviewed when they align with the visual discipline and positioning of Daban-Cêlê.

The brand values long-term aesthetic consistency, clarity of representation, and controlled visibility more than short-term exposure.

Daban-Cêlê is a premium activewear project shaped by a rooted brand character, controlled visual discipline, and a restrained yet confident sporting identity.

Contact

Brand use, editorial requests, and collaboration conversations can be directed through the contact page. Open contact page

Guidelines

Clear ground rules for external brand use

Logo Usage

The Daban-Cêlê name, logo, and visual marks may not appear in digital or printed material without written permission.

Design Language

Visual concept, product presentation, and brand tone are treated as core identity elements that should be respected in collaborations.

Disclaimer

All pieces and collection references shown on the site serve promotional and brand-positioning purposes; collaboration scope is reviewed individually per project.
