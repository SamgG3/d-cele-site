Brand Foundation

A sporting identity shaped through discipline and resistance

Daban-Cêlê was not born from a ready-made legacy. It was shaped by the persistence of two brothers who grew through difficulty.

H. Semih and Melih KARADABAN grew up in the same home and faced the same realities. Resources were limited, but ambition stayed high; that is why the brand was built from resilience rather than comfort.

Daban

Represents standing grounded, drawing strength from the ground beneath you, and holding on to character.

Cêlê

Represents rising higher, pushing your limits, and moving forward day after day.

D-C… the signature of those who do not fall away, do not quit, and keep improving.
