Brand Foundation

The Story of Daban-Cêlê

A brand mindset built to stay grounded and rise higher

Daban-Cêlê was not born from a ready-made legacy. It was shaped by the persistence of two brothers who grew through difficulty.

The Core Meaning
  • Daban: drawing strength from the ground beneath you
  • Cêlê: rising higher and pushing beyond limits
  • D-C: a clean signature for those who do not quit
Stand grounded. Rise higher.

H. Semih and Melih KARADABAN, born in 1997 and 2001, grew up in the same home and shared the same life.

Resources were limited, but their ambition stayed large. From an early age, life taught them effort instead of ease. What others called impossible simply meant a longer road to them.

For them, clothing was never just a necessity. It was a stance. On the street, on the court, in training—wherever movement existed, it became a way to express who you are.

The Language of Motion

Sweat, discipline, and repetition. Going a little further every day. Refusing to stop. Returning stronger. Daban-Cêlê was born from exactly that mindset.

Daban stands for staying grounded and drawing strength from the ground beneath you. Cêlê stands for rising, pushing past your limits, and moving forward without pause.

D-C… in its purest form, the signature of those who do not fall away, do not give up, and keep evolving.

Long Form

A brand built around effort, discipline, movement, and character

This brand was not built overnight. Every piece carries effort, every detail carries discipline, and every design carries motion. Daban-Cêlê stands not only for style, but for strength, endurance, and character.

For Semih and Melih, this is about more than making clothes. It is the story of those who keep improving, push their limits, and choose to begin again every day.

The same idea runs through every detail of the brand: building a sharper, stronger, and more consistent language for activewear.

D-Cêlê… Stand grounded. Rise higher.
