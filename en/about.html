The Story of Daban-Cêlê

A brand story built by two brothers who chose discipline over ease

Daban-Cêlê was not shaped from inherited comfort. It grew from persistence, personal discipline, and a refusal to give up direction or character.

The Core Meaning
  • Daban: standing firm and staying loyal to your roots
  • Cêlê: rising, evolving, and shaping your own path
  • D-C: a signature of endurance and consistency
Prestige born from roots

H. Semih and Melih KARADABAN, born in 1997 and 2001, grew up in the same home and shared the same difficult realities.

What defined their early years was not comfort, but resilience. Instead of following an easy route, they learned how to keep moving through pressure, uncertainty, and long-term effort.

Clothing became more than necessity. It became a form of expression, a way of reflecting identity, discipline, and the raw energy they saw in real life and on the street.

More Than Clothing

Daban stands for grounding, memory, and not losing sight of where you come from. Cêlê stands for elevation, transformation, and the will to define your own route.

D-C, in its simplest form, became the signature of those who stay upright and keep building. The brand was never assembled overnight; it carries patience, repetition, and lived experience in every layer.

Daban-Cêlê was built as a label for strong character, measured ambition, and people who move forward without abandoning their roots.

Long Form

Respect for the past, discipline in the present, and a lasting mark on what comes next

For Semih and Melih, the brand is not only about making garments. It is about honoring the past, carrying the present with discipline, and leaving behind something that keeps its meaning over time.

That is why Daban-Cêlê treats design as more than surface. The collections are shaped to reflect steadiness, intention, and a clear activewear identity with a lasting visual signature.

Every detail is expected to hold the same tone: controlled form, athletic confidence, and a clean sense of purpose.

D-Cêlê is for those who keep rising while staying grounded.
